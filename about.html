Who's behind Cognitix Digital

Cognitix Digital is a one-person creative and technical studio that treats design and engineering as one discipline, not two. Brand identities, posters, mockups and apparel art on one side; websites, software builds and the tech support that keeps a business running on the other.

The thread between them is the same: clear thinking, clean execution, and follow-through after the project ships.
